About Me

Hi I'm Emi, I love origami, crochet, and reading books. I go to the commonwealth school and love music. My favorite book series is The Mysterious Benedict Society. While not reading, or crafting, I can be found participating in plays and performances. The picture below is from the production of Carmen that I was in.

From left to right: The Mysterious Benedict Society, Carmen, stacked crocheted octopuses, assorted origami roses, a completed logic puzzle, and the cast photo for the christmas revels (2014)

From 3rd or 4th grade onward, The Mysterious Benedict Society book series by Trento Lee Stewart was my favorite thing to read. The books are each ~470 pages long, and I'm fairly sure that I've read each of them at least 3 times.

At the beginning of 8th grade, I had the great excitement of performing as a street urchin in George Bizet's opera, Carmen. I'm the one in the aqua headband on the left.
